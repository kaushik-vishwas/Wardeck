What is laser Tag Game?

Laser tag is a recreational shooting sport where participants use infrared-emitting light guns to tag designated targets. LInfrared-sensitive signaling devices are commonly worn by each player to register hits and are sometimes integrated within the arena in which the game is played. These infrared laser beams are completely harmless and no one will get hurt.

    Updated Scenarios

    PHOTONICS headbands change their color depending on the chosen Team: It is now possible to create a team with different color ( Blue Green & White).

    It is also worth paying attention to the Smart PHOTONICS headband background glow function. It is not necessary to use additional devices to mark players from opposing teams. The time of duct tape on weapons and headbands has passed. The PHOTONICS headband offers an elegant solution to those issues.

    Improved cuff design

    The PHOTONICS headband cuff consists of two layers. The first layer is AirMesh. It's made of a three-dimensional weaving grid that forms cells of different sizes in the outer and inner layers. The design ensures durability, constant air circulation and damping properties.

    The second layer is a polyethylene foam sublayer that prevents moisture from entering the outer part of the headband, keeping the electronics and special fabric dry and clean.
